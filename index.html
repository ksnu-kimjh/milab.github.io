Welcome to the Machine Intelligence Lab

Prof. Jeong-Hun Kim, Advisor

At MILab, we value Freedom, Exploration, Sharing, and Growth above all else.

We delve into the fundamental questions of data: discovering hidden patterns within disorder, understanding why they emerge, and using that understanding to create something entirely new. Our vision is to implement machine intelligence to develop technologies that truly benefit the world.

Specifically, we leverage Machine Learning, Computer Vision, and Natural Language Processing to uncover hidden structures across diverse AI convergence fields, striving to overcome the limitations of current artificial intelligence technologies.

This ambitious journey requires colleagues who discuss freely, explore relentlessly, share knowledge openly, and grow together. MILab is a space designed for such passionate researchers. We invite you to join us in this exciting journey.
