Research Field

We explore the fundamental principles of data to discover hidden patterns and implement machine intelligence that benefits the world.

Video Understanding

Developing advanced algorithms to analyze and interpret temporal dynamics in video data for action recognition and event detection.

Representation Learning

Investigating methods to learn meaningful and robust representations of data that capture underlying explanatory factors.

Multimodal Learning

Building systems that can process and relate information from multiple modalities such as text, images, and audio jointly.

World Model for Physical AI

Creating predictive world models that enable physical AI agents to reason about their environment and plan complex actions.

Propose your own research

We value creativity and novel ideas. if you have a unique perspective or a groundbreaking research proposal, we are open to exploration. Join us to turn your innovative concepts into reality.
